📲 Get Notifications

3 quick steps to get push notifications on your phone — even when Dugnad is closed.

1
Install Telegram on your phone if you don't have it already. It's free.
2
Open Telegram and search for @FavorBoardNotifyBot — tap it to open a private chat with the bot.
3
Tap Start, then type and send your Dugnad name — for example:
User 3
The bot will confirm. That's it! 🎉
After setup: You'll receive a private Telegram message every time someone posts a new favor or replies — but never for your own actions.

Privacy: The bot only sends Dugnad notifications. It cannot read your other chats or contacts.
